## 4. Writing Style Rules

### Sentence & Paragraph Structure

- **Sentence length:** [e.g. "Aim for 15–25 words. Vary rhythm — mix short punchy sentences with longer explanatory ones."]
- **Paragraph length:** [e.g. "3–4 sentences for long-form; 1–2 for social and email."]
- **Contractions:** [Yes / No / Depends — explain]
- **Active vs passive voice:** [e.g. "Active by default. Passive only when the actor is unknown or unimportant."]
- **Oxford comma:** [Yes / No]
- **Lists and bullet points:** [e.g. "Use sparingly in long-form content. Acceptable in how-to and product pages."]

### Formatting Preferences

- **Headings:** [e.g. "Sentence case, not Title Case. Action-oriented where possible."]
- **Bold / italics:** [e.g. "Bold for key terms on first use. Italics for emphasis, not for titles."]
- **Numbers:** [e.g. "Spell out one to nine, use numerals for 10+. Always use numerals in data-heavy content."]
- **Dates:** [e.g. "DD Month YYYY (e.g. 15 March 2026). No ordinals."]
- **Currency:** [e.g. "SGD with no space (SGD100), or S$100."]
